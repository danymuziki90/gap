Special focus

GAP SkillAid

A flagship future-of-work platform that equips youth and communities with practical, employable, and entrepreneurial skills.

Objectives

SkillAid bridges unemployment, underemployment, and low access to quality training by creating structured pathways to skills, confidence, and livelihood opportunities.

Employability Entrepreneurship Digital readiness Community resilience

Training Areas

The curriculum can flex by audience and partner, with strong emphasis on hands-on capability.

  • Digital skills

    Foundational digital literacy, productivity, and communication skills.

  • Career readiness

    Professional habits, interview preparation, teamwork, and workplace communication.

  • Entrepreneurial capacity

    Business basics, problem solving, customer thinking, and project execution.

Priority Areas

SkillAid prioritizes participants and communities where training can unlock immediate opportunity or resilience.

Displaced youth Young women Community volunteers Emerging entrepreneurs Post-conflict communities

Partners

The program is designed for collaboration with NGOs, training institutions, employers, funders, and public-facing leadership platforms.

  • Institutions

    Training and academic allies that extend quality and reach.

  • NGOs and civil society

    Delivery and community mobilization partners with trusted local access.

  • Private sector

    Employers and ecosystem allies who help link training to opportunity.

Structure

SkillAid is built as a modular program that can be delivered in cohorts, community hubs, summit-linked intensives, or partner-supported bootcamps.

Cohort intake

Participant selection based on need, readiness, and local context.

Training delivery

Facilitated learning with practical assignments and peer collaboration.

Transition support

Mentorship, placement orientation, or entrepreneurship follow-on guidance.

Impact view

SkillAid contributes to a stronger leadership and livelihoods ecosystem by helping participants become more prepared, more confident, and more connected to opportunity.

Training pathway Practical

Shorter cycles designed for immediate relevance and measurable skill growth.

Audience Youth-first

Especially valuable where disruption has weakened access to formal opportunity.

Value Scalable

Built for replication through institutions, partners, and community hubs.

Outcome Future-ready

Supports employability, initiative, confidence, and resilience at the same time.
